dear friends,

Welcome to my website. My name is Temme and I am probably really excited that you're reading this! (depending on time of day, current general state, etc.) I started writing songs around 14 or 15 and shocker.. I still do! I only ever want to make music that FEELS like something -- can be tragic, joyful, confused, simple and nice, dark and blurry, whatever it may be. I am generally terrified of feeling inauthentic or not genuine, which also ends up making music kind of difficult. I do not spend every minute writing, I don't need to sing all the time, I do not feel furiously and intensely much of the time. My songs come from all different points in my life -- some heavy, some easy, and many somewhere in between. My goal is to bring something to anyone that listens. Maybe you relate, maybe you don't but think you do, maybe you're happy, maybe you hate it. I don't care what it is, so long as it's not nothing.

-Temme
